Bio: I am a Senior Researcher at Tencent AI Lab, Shenzhen, China, where I work with Dr. Jue Wang on Computer Vision. (Last update: Aug 2022)

I did my Ph.D. in the IMAGINE team, École des Ponts ParisTech, advised by Prof. Mathieu Aubry. I also work closely with Prof. Alexei A. Efros.

I graduated from the Master Vision et Apprentissage (MVA, ENS Paris-Saclay) and Engineering Program at Ecole des Ponts ParisTech. Before that, I received dual B.S. degrees in Mechanics from Université Claude Bernard Lyon and Physics from Wuhan University.

Research

I am interested in Computer Vision and Deep Learning. My research mainly focuses on finding solutions to vision tasks without annotations (unsupervised learning) or with easy accessible annotations (weakly supervised learning).
