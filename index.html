About me :

        I am a PhD student in the IMAGINE team, École des Ponts ParisTech, advised by Prof. Mathieu Aubry. I graduated from the Master Vision et Apprentissage (MVA, ENS Paris-Saclay) and Engineering Program at Ecole des Ponts ParisTech. Before that, I received dual B.S. degrees in Mechanics from Université Claude Bernard Lyon and Physics from Wuhan University.

        I am interested in Computer Vision, Deep Learning. My research mainly focuses on finding solutions to image/video tasks without annotations (unsupervised learning) or with easy accessible annotations (weakly supervised learning).
